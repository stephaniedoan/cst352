Adobe Illustrator is the industry-standard vector graphics app that lets you create vibrant graphics, typography, and illustrations for print, web, video, and mobile.
The UI consists of a sidebar that can be dragged to any side of your screen. With a variety of tools to select, create, and manipulate objects or artworks in Illustrator. These tools can be selected as following: drawing, typing, painting, reshaping, slicing and cutting, symbolism, moving and zooming, and graph. Some tools have a small triangle at the bottom right of the toolbox icon. A small triangle has the option to view or expand some hidden tools by hold down the mouse button on the triangle.
